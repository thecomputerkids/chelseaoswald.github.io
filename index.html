About

Fortune Favors Action

My name is Chelsea Oswald, a motivated digital marketing, and front end development student living in the lovely city of Toronto. I am one of four siblings, I love Ted Talks, Brussels Sprouts and I'm currently reading "Valley of the Gods: A Silicon Valley Story" by Alexandra Wolfe.

I have a love for amazing technology and strive to learn something new every day. I value and feel best when I am immersed in authentic and inspiring individuals who encourage confidence and innovation. I thrive off that feeling you get when watch someone speak with great intelligence and passion that it sends shivers through your body and makes you believe you can do absolutely anything you put your mind too.

I spend my days working as an SEO intern at a digital agency. At night I head over to George Brown College where I am currently studying SEO and Adobe Creative Suite. During my time off, I developed The Computer Kids an in-home tutoring service where I teach young children computer coding. For fun, I like to create and re-do websites for friends and family.

If you think I can help you, just want to talk digital over coffee? Reach out. Let's do cool stuff together.
